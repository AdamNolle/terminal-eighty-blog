Christmas 2025

>> 2025-12-25 16:48 | [PERSONAL]

In a world full of fake AI everything, online rage, and distrust in just about everything, be thankful this Christmas for the things in your life that do actually matter: family and friends.

It’s easy to get caught up in the dread of day-to-day life—to think that there isn’t light at the end of the tunnel. But the holidays can remind you of the serenity and peace you can have in your life.

If you celebrate alone, enjoy the peace, or call a friend and just chat. Nothing is better than a simple conversation on the holiday. If you are working, please know how thankful I am, and I am sure many others are, for the sacrifices you are making. If you are with family or friends, enjoy their company and forget the stresses.

Have a very Merry Christmas and a happy holidays!
